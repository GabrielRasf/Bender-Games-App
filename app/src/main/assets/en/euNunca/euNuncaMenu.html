Game Rules

Never Have I Ever
  • Each player must have a glass with their favorite drink.
  • One player at a time will use the phone to read random questions.
  • The player holding the phone taps the "Next Question" button.
  • They read the question out loud to the group.
  • Everyone who has done what is mentioned in the question must take a sip of their drink.
  • The phone is passed to the next player, and the cycle repeats.
