Intimacy - Opening
  • Great game for couples!
  • Each round the couple reads a sentence out loud
  • One will try to guess the other's answer
  • Then the other person will try to guess
  • The goal is to put yourself in the other's shoes
  • And of course, after both answer, you can talk about it!
  • If one player guesses what the other would say, both drink
