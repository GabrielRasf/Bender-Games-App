Game Rules

Secrets
  • The most "shameless" player starts
  • Grab the phone and read a message out loud
  • You must say two options: one false and one true
  • The group must try to guess which story is true
  • If the group guesses right, the liar drinks a shot
  • If the group guesses wrong, the group drinks a shot
  • The game continues clockwise
