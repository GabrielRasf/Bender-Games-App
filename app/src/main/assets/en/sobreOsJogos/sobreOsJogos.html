Never Have I Ever

  • Each player must say "Never have I ever..." followed by something they've never done (or something they have done, if they want to drink).
  • Those who have done the stated action must drink.
  • The goal is to have fun, share funny stories, and get to know the other players better.

Topzeira

  • One person reads an initial topic, like "Most common food allergies in the world".
  • Each player, clockwise, gives an answer. If someone doubts an answer, they can say "That's not topzeira".
  • When this happens, both check the answer in the "star" (the place where you can verify if the answer is true). If it is topzeira, the doubter drinks; if not, the person who gave the wrong answer drinks.

Laugh and Drink

  • A player must perform an action proposed by the card.
  • The goal is to make someone laugh within 30 seconds.
  • If the person laughs, they drink! If you fail to make anyone laugh, you drink.
  • Get ready for jokes, funny faces, and lots of fun!

Secrets

  • Confess something and try to fool others. Say one truth and one lie and let others guess which is which.
  • If others guess the truth, you drink a shot.
  • If they guess wrong, the active player chooses who drinks.
  • It's a fun way to get to know friends better and reveal secrets.

Worst Friends

  • Who's the "Worst Friend" in the group? Point fingers and accuse your friends of crappy behavior—never been so fun!
  • This game will reveal hilarious secrets about your friends.
  • Important! Don't take it to heart—it's just a game.

Adult Charades

  • Perform daring and funny charades for others to guess.
  • The game features creative and fun charades, perfect for those who enjoy funny challenges.
  • It's a chance to loosen up and create hilarious moments!

Truth or Dare

  • Choose: answer honestly or complete a challenge.
  • If you refuse or lie, you must drink.

Who in the Group

  • Example: "Who in the group is the cutest?" – everyone points, and whoever gets the most votes drinks!
  • It’s a game that reveals group opinions in a fun and unexpected way.

To Talk About

  • Deep, random, or funny topics to break the ice.
  • Ideal to start conversations, get to know others better, or have fun with curious subjects.

Guess or Drink

  • If you’re wrong, you drink. Simple and straightforward!
  • This is a quick and direct game with only one rule: guess right, congrats; guess wrong, drink!

What is it?

  • Born from fun, lives in the party, makes you laugh, makes you drink, and nobody complains?
  • Drunk Games, what else could it be?
  • If no one guesses the riddle, everyone drinks!

Unanswered Questions

  • Examples: "Why is the sky blue?", "If time is money, is the bank a clock?".
  • The goal is to raise funny or philosophical questions that no one can answer for sure.
  • If someone tries to answer and the group finds it weak... drinks!

Intimacy

  • Deep questions to discover secrets and unusual stories.
  • Ideal for couples to deepen their relationship with more openness and trust.

Drunk Show

  • A crazy quiz with bizarre questions. Whoever gets it wrong drinks!
  • Get ready to laugh and be surprised by the questions.

Didn't Do It? Drink

  • Challenges or tasks are proposed.
  • Whoever doesn't do it must drink. Simple as that!

Target Shooting

  • 4 questions, one answer for each question.
  • The person with the highest score wins.
  • The person with the lowest score drinks!

Fact or False

  • Can you tell if what you know is a fact or false?
  • In this game, you will be presented with various pieces of knowledge.
  • If you get right what is fact and what is false, congratulations.
  • If you get it wrong, you know what happens!

Luck or Bad Luck

  • Here you choose one, two, or three numbers between 1 and 6
  • Click the button and, if it lands on any chosen number, you know what to do. Drink
  • Take a shot
  • Game of pure luck (or bad luck)... laughter guaranteed!

Before or After

  • Two historical facts.
  • One chance.
  • If wrong, drink.
  • If right, congratulations!
