  • Players ko do teams mein divide karo.
  • Har round mein, ek team apna ek player choose karega jo mimics karega.
  • Wo banda ek mimic padhega aur apni team ke liye act karega.
  • 60 seconds ka timer start karo aur mimic karna shuru karo.
  • Us player ki team ko card ka content guess karna hoga.
  • Agar team sahi guess karti hai, toh ek point milega.
  • Agar team galat guess kare, toh ek player ek shot lega.
  • Next round mein dusri team khelti hai.
  • Jo team zyada points leti hai, wo jeetegi.
  • Variation
  • Mimic karne wala player sabke liye karega.
  • Jo players kam se kam sahi jawab denge specified rounds mein, wo ek shot lenge.
