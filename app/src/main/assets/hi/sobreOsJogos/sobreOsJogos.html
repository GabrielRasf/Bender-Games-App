Pehle ya Baad Mein?

Eu Nunca

  • Har player kahega "Eu nunca..." aur fir kuch aisa jo usne kabhi nahi kiya (ya jo kiya ho, agar peena hai toh).
  • Jo player jo baat kahi gayi hai wo kar chuka hai, use peena padega.
  • Maqsad hai masti karna, mazedar stories share karna aur apne doston ko acche se jaana.

Topzeira

  • Ek aadmi shuruat karta hai ek topic se, jaise "Duniya ke sabse common food allergies".
  • Har player clockwise jawab deta hai. Agar koi answer par doubt karta hai, toh "Not topzeira" bol sakta hai.
  • Phir dono answers ko "star" pe check karte hain. Agar jawab sahi hai toh doubt karne wale ko peena padega; galat jawab dene wale ko peena padega.

Hansa, Piya

  • Ek player karta hai action jo card pe likha hota hai.
  • Maqsad hai kisi ko 30 seconds mein hasana.
  • Agar koi hasta hai, toh wo peeta hai! Agar aap kisi ko nahi hasa pate, toh aapko peena padega.
  • Tayaar ho jao jokes, funny faces aur full masti ke liye!

Secrets

  • Kuch sach bolo aur kuch jhoot bhi. Baaki log guess karenge ki sach kya hai aur jhoot kya.
  • Agar baaki log sach guess kar lein, toh aapko peena padega.
  • Agar galat guess karein, toh active player decide karta hai ki kaun peega.
  • Masti se apne doston ko better jaanein aur secrets share karein.

Worst Dost

  • Group ka "Worst Dost" kaun hai? Ungli uthao aur doston ko unki bakwaas harkaton ke liye accuse karo — full comedy!
  • Ye game reveal karega doston ke funny secrets aur embarrassing moments.
  • Important hai! Dil pe mat lena yaar, bas ek game hai.

Adult Mimics

  • Doston ko guesses dene ke liye bold aur funny mimics karo.
  • Ye game funny challenges deta hai jo aapko full masti karne ka chance deta hai.
  • Ek accha mauka hai masti karne aur funny moments create karne ka!

Truth or Dare

  • Choose: sach bolo ya challenge complete karo.
  • Agar mana karo ya jhoot bolo, toh peena padega.

Who in the Group

  • Example: "Group mein sabse cute kaun hai?" Sab point karenge, jispe sabse zyada votes aayenge wo peega.
  • Ye game group ki opinions funny tareeke se reveal karta hai.

To Talk

  • Deep, random ya funny topics jo ice-break karte hain.
  • Perfect hai conversations start karne ke liye, dost ko achhe se jaan-ne ke liye ya masti ke liye.

Hit or Drink

  • Jo galat answer dega, use peena padega. Simple!
  • Ye fast-paced game hai jisme sirf ek rule hai: sahi answer do ya peeo!

What is it?

  • Zoeira se paida hua, masti mein jeeta, haste haste peeta aur koi shikayat nahi karta?
  • Bebedeira Games, aur kya ho sakta hai?
  • Agar koi question ka sahi jawab nahi de paya, sabko peena hai!

Unanswered Questions

  • Examples: "Asmaan neela kyun hai?", "Agar time paisa hai toh bank kya hai?"
  • Maqsad hai funny ya philosophical questions uthana jo koi clearly jawab nahi de sakta.
  • Agar koi jawab dene ki koshish kare aur sabko weak lage... peena padega!

Intimacy

  • Deep questions jo secrets aur unusual stories reveal karte hain.
  • Couples ke liye perfect, relationship ko aur strong aur trusting banane ke liye.

Show of Bebão

  • Crazy quiz with weird questions. Jo galat answer dega, wo peega!
  • Tayaar ho jao hasne aur surprised hone ke liye!

Nahin kiya? Peena padega

  • Challenges ya tasks diye jaate hain.
  • Jo nahi karta, use peena padega. Bas itna simple!

Target Shot

  • 4 questions, har question ke liye ek answer.
  • Jo sabse zyada score karega, wo jeetega.
  • Jo sabse kam score karega, wo peega!

Fact or Fake

  • Kya aap bata sakte ho ki jo aap jaante ho wo fact hai ya fake?
  • Is game mein, aapko bahut saari cheezein pata chalengi.
  • Agar aap fact aur fake dono sahi pehchaano, toh badhai ho!
  • Agar galat karo, toh... peena padega!

Luck or Bad Luck

  • Yahan aap 1 se 6 ke beech 1, 2 ya 3 numbers choose karte ho.
  • Button dabao aur agar number aapke chosen numbers mein aaye, toh aapko pata hai na, peena padega.
  • Ek drink lo.
  • Pura luck ka game (ya bad luck)... full laughs guaranteed!

Before or After

  • Do historical facts diye jaate hain.
  • Ek chance hota hai decide karne ka.
  • Agar galat karo, toh peena padega.
  • Agar sahi karo, toh congrats!
